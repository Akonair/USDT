Bank Direct Deposit Steps (VCB Example)

Description: VCB deposit steps, including login, confirming details, and confirming payment.

  1. Log in to VCB Banking Service: Open the VCB banking app or website, navigate to the “Deposit” page.
  2. Confirm Deposit Details: Verify the transaction code (e.g., 786606426731405312), amount (e.g., 100,000 VND), and beneficiary information in the deposit form.
  3. Confirm via SMS or OTP: Click “Đăng Nhập” (Log In) and enter your SMS/OTP code for identity verification.
  4. Complete the Deposit: Follow the prompts to finalize the payment.

Important Reminder: Ensure deposits are made directly by the account holder to avoid delays or rejections due to third-party payments.

QR Code Deposit Steps (QR Code Example)

  1. Open Any Mobile Banking App: Launch any mobile banking app on your smartphone.
  2. Scan the QR Code: Tap “QR Pay” to scan the provided QR code (amount: 100,000 VND).
  3. Confirm Payment: Verify transaction details (e.g., recipient, amount), then click to confirm and complete the payment.

Important Tip: Avoid repeatedly scanning the QR code to prevent duplicate payments. After each transaction, check your account balance and save the transaction record.
